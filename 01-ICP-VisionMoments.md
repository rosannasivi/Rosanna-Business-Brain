# 01 · Ideal Client Profile (ICP)
**Rosanna Sivillica · VisionMoments**
*Purely Personal · by Daniel Paul*

---

## Who they are
Professionals and businesses in the finance, banking, and real estate sectors — as well as private individuals — who want to create a memorable event but lack the time to organise it themselves.

## Day-to-day situation
Busy executives, entrepreneurs, and families who are clear on what they want but cannot dedicate the time or energy to coordinate every detail. They operate in high-standards environments and expect the same from their suppliers.

## What they are quietly desperate to solve
They want an event that feels exactly right — for employees, clients, or family — but every attempt to delegate or DIY it has left them disappointed because nobody truly understood their vision.

## What makes them a perfect fit
They give clear instructions and defined deadlines. They trust the expert once the brief is agreed and do not micromanage. They value relationships built on discretion and results.

## Primary channels to reach them
- LinkedIn (banking / finance network)
- Personal referrals
- WhatsApp community
- Instagram and Facebook (private network)
